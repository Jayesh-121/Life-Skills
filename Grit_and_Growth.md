# Grit and Growth Mindset

## 1. Grit – Video Summary

The perfomance of a person in any given field is directly linked to grit rather than IQ or innate skill. It implies that consistent long term perseverance despite repeated failure is more important than natural talent. 

---

## 2. Growth Mindset
Growth mindset means believing your ability to do something is not completely dependant on innate talent but also on the consistent effort put in to improve at it over time. It encourages embracing the learning process and accepting critique and challenges rather than avoiding them. 

---

## 3. Internal Locus of Control
- Internal Locus of Control is the belief that your life outcomes are dictated by your actions rather than luck or external factors.

- When you take responsibility for your results, you feel more motivated and become more effective in improving yourself.

---

## 4. How to Build a Growth Mindset – Key Points
- Embrace challenges  
- Learn from criticism  
- Celebrate effort  
- Persist through difficulties  
- Focus on learning, not just results  
- Believe improvement is possible  
- Practice consistently  

---

## 5. Ideas to Take Action & Build Growth Mindset
- Set realistic daily learning goals 
- Reflect daily on what was learned  
- Seek feedback regularly  
- Try tasks outside your comfort zone  
- Track progress to stay motivated  
- Embrace mistakes as part of learning  
- Have a positive outlook despite possible failure
